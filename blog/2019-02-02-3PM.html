Adding images to cards

I found a wonderful website that lists images that are in the public domain. They happend to have a section with fantasy art.

So, I looked them through and decided upon a few images that I thought would be great to add some flavor to the otherwise very plain user interface. They worked out great so now all cards have a background that communicates what the faction is. I think it looks great and makes the game a little nicer to look at.
