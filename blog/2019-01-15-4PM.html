Custom team names and some clarity

A lot of feedback I have received has been about the interface being unclear. So, today I have spent some time making some things clearer.

I added a little widget next to the initiative order that tells you whose team's turn it is, which monster's turn it is and what the current round is. There is also a button to show smaller versions of the monster cards, if you think the cards take up too much space.

I also added the cost to monster cards so you know before you are buying a monster how much it costs. It is very strange that I didn't add that to begin with.

You can also give the team you are creating a name. That name will then be used during the battle to display whose team's turn it is.

Other interface changes include: Buttons are looks the same, Back to lobby button in team select, buttons don't have unexpected behavior.
